About Us

Stories Behind Local Businesses is about showing how everyday, working class businesses help shape the communities around them. Places like cafés, restaurants, local shops, and small services aren't just there for convenience, they're where people meet, talk, and build real connection.

As things become more digital, these kinds of interactions are starting to fade. This project focuses on capturing what makes these spaces important by talking to business owners, documenting their environments, and exploring the relationships that grow through them.

Through interviews, visual documentation, and personal messages, we capture the unique relationships that form within these spaces. By sharing these stories, we hope to encourage greater appreciation, support, and recognition of the small businesses that quietly strengthen the social fabric of our communities.

DIGITALISATION

Inside Local Businesses

Digitalisation is having a noticeable impact on local businesses, changing the way they operate and connect with their customers. Many small businesses are now expected to maintain an online presence through social media, websites, and digital platforms, which can help them reach a wider audience but also adds pressure to stay constantly active and competitive. While this shift can improve visibility and convenience, it can also reduce the level of face to face interaction that has traditionally been a key part of local business and community life. As a result, some businesses are adapting quickly to stay relevant, while others struggle to balance digital demands with maintaining their original identity and personal relationships with customers.

The photography alongside this documents these spaces in their everyday form, capturing their atmosphere, details, and character as physical places that still rely on real world connection, showing the contrast between their traditional presence and the growing influence of the digital world.

INTERVIEW AND DOCUMENTARY

Conversations in Change

The interview and documentary section focuses on people in their working environments as they go about their daily roles alongside modern technology. It features real conversations with individuals, sharing their thoughts and experiences of digitalisation in the workplace.

Through these interviews, people talk openly about how digital tools and platforms are changing the way they work, from daily routines to customer interactions. Many reflect on how these changes affect their livelihoods, while also discussing the challenges of adapting to new systems and expectations.

The documentary aims to give an honest insight into how different people are experiencing this shift, capturing both the pressures and the ways they continue to adapt within their working lives.
